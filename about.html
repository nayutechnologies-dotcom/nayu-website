Our Story

We're Building the AI-Native Enterprise

Nayu Technologies was founded on a simple belief: every enterprise deserves access to the best AI technology and the expertise to make it work in the real world.

Our Mission

Democratizing Enterprise AI

Nayu Technologies Inc. is an AI & automation consulting firm headquartered in Toronto, Canada. We help enterprises in financial services and beyond harness the transformative power of intelligent technology.

We believe AI is not just a technology upgrade — it's a fundamental shift in how organizations operate, compete, and serve their customers. Our mission is to guide enterprises through this shift with clarity, speed, and accountability.

Our team combines deep technical expertise in AI/ML with real domain knowledge in financial services — so we don't just build AI, we build AI that actually solves the problems your business faces.
